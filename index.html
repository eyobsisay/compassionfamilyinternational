Who we are

Compassion Family International is Ethiopia based local nongovernmental organization established in April 2008 to assist low income families, orphans and other vulnerable member of the community.

What we do

We provide integrated support like meals, clothing, medical care, and educational resources for low-income families and orphan children. We also empower low income families with technical trainings, financial access for their start ups.

How we do it

We work through donations, partnerships, and volunteers. Donations fund our projects, partners share our goals, and volunteers help us achieve them.
what We Do
Every act of kindness matters. We approach community work with care, love, and passion. When our community faces challenges, we take action and set an example for others to follow. By demonstrating compassion and proactive support, we inspire others to join us in making a positive impact, showing that together, we can overcome any obstacle.

"Empowering Every Need, Enriching Every Life: Compassion in Action" At Compassion, we're dedicated to supporting students from low-income families and government schools. We provide daily lunches during school days to ensure students can focus on their education and achieve success in life. Thanks to the generous contributions from our donors, we're able to consistently provide lunches to our targeted students. In addition to our lunch program, Compassion identifies low-income families and offers in-kind support. We conduct home-to-home visits to deliver a range of social services to these communities. Through these interventions, we aim to empower communities and foster resilience. Furthermore, Compassion facilitates access to resources that help families generate income. By doing so, we not only empower families but also strengthen the communities we serve.
