About Us

Please Rise your Helping Hand

"Empowering Every Need, Enriching Every Life: Compassion in Action" At Compassion, we're dedicated to supporting students from low-income families and government schools. We provide daily lunches during school days to ensure students can focus on their education and achieve success in life. Thanks to the generous contributions from our donors, we're able to consistently provide lunches to our targeted students. In addition to our lunch program, Compassion identifies low-income families and offers in-kind support. We conduct home-to-home visits to deliver a range of social services to these communities. Through these interventions, we aim to empower communities and foster resilience. Furthermore, Compassion facilitates access to resources that help families generate income. By doing so, we not only empower families but also strengthen the communities we serve.

Our Mission

To empower low-income families, orphans, and other vulnerable members of our community. Compassion (CFI) provides essential support and resources to those in need. We focus on delivering necessities and comprehensive assistance to student children and their families. Through integrated support packages, we aim to empower individuals to shape their own futures to a positive direction.

Our Vision

Our aim is to uplift orphans, vulnerable children, and the parents or guardians in our community in Addis Ababa, restoring their dignity, self-respect, and sense of value in life. We are committed to providing comprehensive support that encompasses physical, educational, psychosocial care, and economic empowerment. We also strive to assist them in becoming active contributors to society, ensuring they thrive and positively impact their communities.

Our Values

Our Values is to uplift orphans, vulnerable children, and their parents or guardians in Addis Ababa by restoring their dignity, self-respect, and sense of value in life. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive support that includes physical, educational, and psychosocial care, as well as economic empowerment. We strive to help them become active contributors to society, ensuring they thrive and positively impact their communities.
