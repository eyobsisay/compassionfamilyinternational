Our Process

How do You Help?

We are thrilled to extend our warmest invitation to you to join our compassionate team of volunteers in supporting our ongoing activities aimed at making a positive impact in communities. At Compassion Family International, we believe in the power of collective action and the profound difference that individuals like you can make in the lives of others.

Lunch Serving Assistance

Volunteers play a vital role in ensuring that nutritious meals are served to those in need. By assisting with lunch serving, you'll have the opportunity to directly impact the well-being of individuals in our community. Whether it's helping in the kitchen, setting up serving stations, or engaging with guests, your support is invaluable in creating a welcoming and nourishing environment for all.
Donate Now

In-Home Support and Visits

Our volunteers provide invaluable companionship and assistance to individuals and families in their homes. Whether it's offering a listening ear, running errands, or providing practical support, your presence can bring comfort and hope to those facing challenging circumstances. Through home visits, you'll have the chance to build meaningful connections and make a lasting difference in the lives of others.
Join Us Now

Empowering Communities

We believe in empowering communities to create sustainable change from within. As a volunteer, you can play a key role in facilitating workshops, training sessions, and other initiatives aimed at empowering individuals to generate income and build a brighter future for themselves and their families. Whether it's teaching valuable skills, providing mentorship, or facilitating resource-sharing, your support can help unlock the potential of communities and foster long-term growth and resilience.

"Empowering Every Need, Enriching Every Life: Compassion in Action" At Compassion, we're dedicated to supporting students from low-income families and government schools. We provide daily lunches during school days to ensure students can focus on their education and achieve success in life. Thanks to the generous contributions from our donors, we're able to consistently provide lunches to our targeted students. In addition to our lunch program, Compassion identifies low-income families and offers in-kind support. We conduct home-to-home visits to deliver a range of social services to these communities. Through these interventions, we aim to empower communities and foster resilience. Furthermore, Compassion facilitates access to resources that help families generate income. By doing so, we not only empower families but also strengthen the communities we serve.
